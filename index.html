Got questions?

What's the daily schedule like?

Mornings start with a gentle wake-up and breakfast. Midday brings activities and rest. Evenings are for quiet time and dinner. We aim for a predictable, peaceful flow.

How do I ask for help?

Just press the call button by your bed! Our friendly staff are always nearby and ready to assist you with anything you need, big or small.

Can my family visit?

Absolutely! We encourage visits. Please check our visitor guidelines for specific times to keep everyone comfortable and safe.

What if I disagree with a decision?

Your voice matters! You can discuss concerns with your doctor or nurse. We have patient advocates ready to help mediate and clarify.

Are there special services for caregivers?

Yes! We offer resources and support for those caring for our patients. Ask our staff about caregiver support groups and information sessions.
